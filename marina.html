MARINA BEACH

Marina Beach, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is one of the longest natural urban beaches in the world. Stretching over 13 kilometers, it is a popular destination for both locals and tourists. The beach is known for its golden sands, gentle waves, and vibrant atmosphere. Marina Beach is not just a place to relax; it's a cultural hub with various activities and attractions. From horse rides and kite flying to enjoying local street food like sundal and bhajji, there's something for everyone. The beach is also home to several historical monuments and statues, including the iconic Anna Memorial and the Triumph of Labour statue.
