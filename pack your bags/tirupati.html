TIRUPATI

Tirupati, located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, is one of the most revered pilgrimage destinations in India. It is home to the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The temple is situated on the Tirumala Hills, part of the Eastern Ghats, and attracts millions of devotees annually. Tirupati is not just a spiritual hub but also a place of natural beauty, with lush green hills, waterfalls, and serene landscapes. The Tirupati Laddu, offered as prasadam at the temple, is famous worldwide and has a Geographical Indication (GI) tag. The city is well-connected by road, rail, and air, with the Tirupati Airport providing easy access. Festivals like Brahmotsavam and Rathasapthami are celebrated with great fervor, drawing devotees from across the globe.
