MANALI

Located in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is a picturesque hill station known for its snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and adventure sports. The town is often referred to as the Valley of Gods due to its stunning natural beauty and spiritual significance. Manali is home to attractions like Solang Valley, Hadimba Temple, and Rohtang Pass, which offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas. The best time to visit Manali is from October to June, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. Whether you're interested in trekking, skiing, or simply soaking in the mesmerizing views, Manali has something for every traveler!

Manali is a haven for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. The Beas River offers opportunities for river rafting, while the Great Himalayan National Park is perfect for trekking and wildlife spotting.

üåø Adventure Sports and Activities

Manali is famous for its adventure sports, including skiing, paragliding, and zorbing. The Solang Valley is a hub for these activities, attracting thrill-seekers from all over the world.

Manali offers numerous trekking trails, including the Hampta Pass Trek and Beas Kund Trek, which provide stunning views of the Himalayas.
